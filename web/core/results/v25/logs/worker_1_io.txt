# Master Architect's Specific Prompt For You
You are an Algorithmic Logic Architect evolving a heads-up No-Limit Texas Hold'em bot. You are branching from claude_v9 (the TOP-rated bot at r=1649.9), NOT from v24. Your mission is to fix a CRITICAL structural flaw: the bot is a preflop nit (folds 55% of hands) combined with a postflop calling station (0% fold on flop and turn). This is catastrophic in HU poker.

Read bots/claude_v9/main.py FIRST to understand the current codebase you're working from.

## PRIMARY OBJECTIVE: Invert the Fold-Aggression Profile

### Task 1: Widen Preflop Range (MOST IMPORTANT)
Current problem: The bot folds 54-55% of hands preflop in HU. In heads-up poker, you should be playing 65-70%+ of hands from the button/small blind.

Implement a preflop hand strength evaluation that classifies hands into tiers:
- **Tier 1 (Top 10%)**: AA-TT, AKs-AJs, AKo → Always raise 3-4x BB
- **Tier 2 (Next 15%)**: 99-77, ATs-KTs, AQo-ATo, KQo → Raise 2.5-3x BB
- **Tier 3 (Next 25%)**: 66-22, suited connectors 54s+, suited gappers T8s+, broadway suited QJs+, any ace A2s+ → Raise 2-2.5x BB or call
- **Tier 4 (Next 15%)**: Offsuit connectors 54o+, suited 2-gappers, weak aces A5o-A2o → Call (complete the SB or check BB)
- **Tier 5 (Bottom 35%)**: Truly unplayable trash → Fold ONLY these

Key insight: The preflop fold rate should drop from 55% to ~30%. Only the worst 30% of hands should be folded.

### Task 2: Add Postflop Folding Logic (CRITICAL)
Current problem: 0% fold rate on flop AND turn. The bot never lets go postflop, allowing opponents to value-bet relentlessly for -15000 to -20000 chip losses.

Add a `should_fold_postflop()` function that evaluates:
1. **Board texture threat**: Count completed draws (flush draws completed on turn/river, straight possibilities). If 3+ threats on board and hand is weak → fold.
2. **Opponent bet sizing**: If opponent bets >75% pot AND our hand strength (estimated equity) < 0.35 → fold. This prevents paying off obvious value bets.
3. **Multi-street aggression**: If opponent has bet/raised on 2+ consecutive streets AND our hand hasn't improved → fold.
4. **Made hand strength**: Classify our hand as: air (nothing), weak pair, medium pair (top pair with weak kicker), strong (two pair+). Only fold air and weak pairs against large bets.

Target fold rates: Flop 15-25%, Turn 20-30%, River 10-15%.

### Task 3: Increase Bet Sizing
Current problem: Average raise is 0.3-0.5x pot — way too small. Opponents can profitably call with any draw.

Modify the raise sizing logic:
- **With strong hands (two pair+)**: Bet 70-100% pot (pot-sized for protection and value)
- **With medium hands (top pair good kicker)**: Bet 50-75% pot
- **Bluffs/semi-bluffs**: Bet 60-80% pot (need fold equity)
- **Continuation bets on dry boards**: 40-50% pot
- **Continuation bets on wet boards**: 60-75% pot (charge draws)

### IMPLEMENTATION RULES:
- You are editing bots/claude_v9/main.py (the source you branch from). Do NOT touch any other files.
- Add functions for preflop hand tiering, postflop fold decisions, and bet sizing. Integrate them into the existing decision flow.
- Keep total file under 1000 lines.

- Focus on STRUCTURAL changes: new functions, new evaluation logic, new decision branches.
